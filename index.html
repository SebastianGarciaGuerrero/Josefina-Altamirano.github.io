About Me

Josefina is a Senior Specialist Speech and Language Therapist who graduated from Gabriela Mistral University, Chile. After working a few months in the public health started a new challenge in private practice. Josefina have worked private clinic for more than 9 years as well a schools with inclusive programs, has extensive experience working with children and families from different cultural backgrounds.

She had a varied caseload in schools with pre-schoolers and has considerable experience working with children with a range of speech, language and communication difficulties including Autism Spectrum Disorder, Downs Syndrome, Hearing Impairment, Swallowing Disorders, Language Delay, and Speech and Phonological Difficulties. She particularly enjoys working with nursery and pre-school aged children.

Josefina is also senior Hearing Specialist, and she provides Hearing and Vestibular Consultations at a medical center. She offers, innovative exercises and makes effective use of various cutting edge technologies for treatment of disturbed vestibular function, dizziness and a vulnerability to falls especially in the over 55s. As a result of his various methods and Josefina provides great functional gains for his patients. A wide range of patients with a variety of conditions are assess and treat. These conditions include BPPV, Labyrinthitis, Migraine, Visual vertigo, Vestibulopathy.

She is currently working in a medical center, as well as with private clients from her own therapy center that are based in Santiago.
